# Privacy Policy — Tamaninah App

**Last updated:** Sunday, March 8, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Tamaninah**. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

Tamaninah **does not collect any personal data** that directly identifies you. We may use location data solely to calculate prayer times and the Qibla direction. This data is never stored or shared with any third party. All your data is stored **locally on your device only**.

---

## 3. Location Data

The app uses location access to calculate accurate prayer times and the Qibla direction. Your location is never shared with any third party and remains on your device only.

---

## 4. Notifications

The app may request permission to send notifications to remind you of prayer times and Adhkar. You can revoke this permission at any time from your **device settings**.

---

## 5. Locally Stored Data

The app stores your settings and progress data (such as achievements and prayer logs) locally on your device only. None of this data is uploaded to external servers.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Tamaninah does not rely on any third-party analytics services or advertising platforms that collect your data.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all age groups and does not intentionally collect data from children under 13.

---

## 8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified of any material changes through the app. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
